About Us

At Bangkok Bachata Gang (BBG), we're more than just a dance school - we're a community of passionate dancers and instructors dedicated to spreading the joy of Latin dance in Thailand. Founded in 2023, we've rapidly grown to become Bangkok's premiere destination for bachata, salsa, and kizomba instruction. 120+ students a month and growing can attest to that! We're big on community, as we host multiple practices a week and a monthly bonding event.

But you don't have to be a dancer, have rhythm have a partner, or have experience to join us. We pride ourselves on being open, positive, non-judgmental, lighthearted, and supportive. There are no egos here, and we don't tolerate mean people. Come for dance, leave with community and lifelong friendships for both beginner and advanced dancers!
